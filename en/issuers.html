U.S. SMART Health Card Issuers

Proof of vaccination is important to be able to get back to life safely, which is why there are hundreds of clinical and government entities in the United States and worldwide issuing SMART Health Cardsâ€¦and the list is growing every day.

But for all of us to have confidence in each other's vaccination status, it is critical that we trust these issuers, which is why there needs to be a publicly available list of approved issuers. The VCI Directory does just that. The SMART Health Cards team has worked with the VCI coalition to prepare this directory. Its sole purpose is to make sure organizations accepting SMART Health Cards have a reliable source they can trust when they are verifying these cards.

The following organizations have been validated and all are approved as SMART Health Cards issuers in the VCI Directory, including pharmacies, labs, hospitals, clinicians, and public health departments.
