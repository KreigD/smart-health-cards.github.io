Find My Issuer

Select one of the locations where you were vaccinated to see SMART Health Cards issuers in your area.

All locations that issue SMART Health Cards have a icon next to them below. If you were vaccinated in more than one state, territory, or country and only one of them is a SMART Health Card issuer, please select that location. If no icon appears next to the location where you were vaccinated your state, you still may be able to get a SMART Health Card through pharmacies and healthcare providers within the state. Those options will be listed after you select your state.
